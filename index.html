About Me

My name is Jae Jin Kim, but most people call me JJ. I was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea, and now call Houston, Texas, my home. My journey to the U.S. began when I started my studies at BYU in Provo, and over time, my family made the move with me. Today, I work as a researcher in the energy industry, where I explore innovative solutions for the future. I have a deep passion for learning new things, whether it is expanding my knowledge in my field or diving into personal interests like web development. The thrill of solving problems and creating something new keeps me inspired every day.

Seoul, South Korea

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is home to over 9.4 million people, making it one of the largest cities in the world. It is a global center for technology, fashion, and entertainment, with famous landmarks like Gyeongbokgung Palace and the N Seoul Tower. The city seamlessly blends history and modernity, from traditional hanok villages to futuristic skyscrapers. With an extensive subway system and a metropolitan area housing over 25 million people, Seoul is one of the most connected and dynamic cities on the planet.
