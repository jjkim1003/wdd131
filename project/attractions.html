Top Attractions in Seoul

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Step back in time at Seoul’s most iconic royal palace, where history meets tradition with guard ceremonies and Hanbok photo ops.

N Seoul Tower

Catch a panoramic view of the entire city from this romantic landmark atop Namsan Mountain — don’t forget the love locks!

Bukchon Hanok Village

Stroll through traditional Korean houses (hanok) and alleyways preserved amidst the modern cityscape.

Han River Parks

Enjoy biking, picnicking, or sunset views at Seoul’s famous riverside parks—popular with locals and tourists alike.

Dongdaemun Design Plaza

Marvel at futuristic architecture, fashion shows, and exhibits in Seoul’s design and innovation hub.

Lotte World Tower

Visit the world’s 5th tallest skyscraper, complete with skywalk, luxury mall, aquarium, and observation deck.

Insadong Street

Explore Seoul's cultural heart filled with traditional crafts, tea houses, calligraphy, and antique shops.

Hongdae District

Known for its vibrant youth culture, street art, indie music, cafes, and late-night food spots near Hongik Univ.

COEX Mall & Starfield Library

Asia’s largest underground shopping mall featuring an iconic open-concept library, aquarium, and global shopping.
