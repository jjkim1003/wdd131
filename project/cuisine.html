Delicious Korean Dishes

Kimchi

Fermented spicy cabbage is Korea’s most iconic side dish, packed with flavor and probiotics.

Bibimbap

A colorful rice bowl topped with assorted vegetables, meat, a fried egg, and spicy gochujang sauce.

Bulgogi

Thinly sliced marinated beef, grilled to perfection, often wrapped in lettuce with garlic and ssamjang.

Tteokbokki

Chewy rice cakes in a fiery red chili sauce — a favorite Korean street food with a spicy kick.

Samgyeopsal

Grilled pork belly slices served with dipping sauces, lettuce, and side dishes — a classic Korean BBQ.

Haemul Pajeon

Seafood scallion pancake, crispy on the outside and perfect for sharing — especially on a rainy day.

Soondubu Jjigae

A spicy soft tofu stew with seafood or meat, served bubbling hot in a stone pot. Comfort food at its best!

Galbi

Sweet and savory marinated beef short ribs, grilled to perfection and commonly served at Korean BBQ restaurants.

Hotteok

Sweet Korean pancakes filled with brown sugar, cinnamon, and crushed nuts — perfect as a warm winter street snack.
